# Pizza-sales-Analysis-using-Power-bi
This project focuses on analyzing pizza sales data to uncover key business insights related to revenue performance, customer demand, and product popularityy. Using Power BI, the analysis explores sales trends across time (monthly and quarterly), evaluates pizza categories and sizes, and identifies top-performing and underperforming products. The dashboard highlights critical KPIs such as total revenue, total orders, average order value, and pizzas per order, enabling a clear understanding of customer purchasing behavior. Special emphasis is placed on identifying high-demand items and designing data-driven strategies like bundling and upselling to improve order volume and revenue. This project demonstrates the use of DAX measures, data modeling, and interactive visualizations to support business decision-making and showcase practical analytical thinking

OBJECTIVE 
* To derive actionable insights that support sales growth, product optimization, and strategic decision-making.
* To assess customer demand patterns by examining total orders.
* To identify top-selling and low-performing pizzas based on total orders and revenue contribution.
* To analyse quarterly and monthly revenue performance and identify peak and low-performing periods.
* To analyze pizza sales data to identify trends in revenue, orders, and customer preferences across categories and sizes.
